Hi! I am Mudra, a UX/UI Designer and aspiring HCI practitioner.

Presently, I am a UX Designer at VuNet Systems, Bangalore India. I have completed my Bachelors in Computer Engineering from Dwarkadas J Sanghvi College of Engineering (affiliated with the University of Mumbai), Mumbai, India.

My interest in design goes all the way back to grade 7 when I finished reading ‘Steve Jobs’ by Walter Isaacson. It wasn’t until my undergraduate that I discovered UX/UI Design while working on a user-centred project at Unicode. Since then, I have worked at Medtronic, Felix Educare and TribesForGood. I have also been the Design & HR Head at Unicode from 2017 to 2020.

In my free time, I like to read about personality psychology, behavioural science and sustainability in design.

I enjoy writing and I believe it is an important skill across all careers. You can read some of my work here.

I also love challenges! My most recent challenge was Inktober2019, where every October, artists all over the world make one ink drawing a day for the entire month.

I am fueled by group discussions. Some of my favourite things to talk about at - ethics and technology, how to make better teams, do designers need to code?, and the eco guilt associated with plastic packaging.

You can download my resume here, write to me at mudranagda@gmail.com. Alternatively, you can add me on Linkedin to receive updates about my work.

Thanks for stopping by!
