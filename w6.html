// soul searching //

Look within
Open your eyes
Not this face, these hands, this body
But the essence of what makes you you
You'll find it in different places
in different dimensions
every piece you pick up
examine, feel
some will have thorns
that hurt your soul not your skin
they're the weight your heart is bearing
lift them with all your might
and throw it out of your sight
For the ones you've watered and watered and watered
beaming off the fresh sunlight
these are the strengths you know of
salute to their presence
for they have served their tenure
For the ones you'll find
shining in crevices
glistening in the dark
sometimes wearing the cloak of invisibility
find them
find all of them
and give me them a long deserving hug
for they've stayed by your side
on low nights and days of self doubt
when you didn't know what you were
or what you were going to do
They somehow paved the way through
