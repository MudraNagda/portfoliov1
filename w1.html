// identity //

One of the many important things I've learnt in the past 21 years of existence, is that it is absolutely okay to be happy, even if life doesn't love you everytime. Its okay to say no. It's okay to walk out of things that do not excite you. It's okay not to be on social media for years, if it makes you feel like you'd rather be collecting memories than likes. It's okay to say you'd rather stay at home laughing it out with your fam, than go to a party to "turn up". It's okay to be a child at heart. It's okay if you do not like make up. It's okay, and not creepy to genuinely ask people how their heart is feeling, and give them a huge hug if things aren't too sunny, even if they're cold and distant in return. It's okay to give more than just one chance, (but if they ask for more, they blew it), it's okay if you think so deeply that you get labelled over-dramatic when you're angry. It's okay to NOT feel bad for yourself if nothing is working out, you just get up, dust off, move on relentlessly. It's okay if you haven't watched every new TV show, and it's okay if LIT AF or MOOD is not in your vocabulary (you are your own kind of lit). It's okay to care for your people so much that they're sick and tired of your motherlyness. It's okay to vehemently disagree to popular opinion and state your own. It's okay to get hurt and cry, as long as the tears dry off. It's okay to say you're sorry, even if you've done nothing wrong. It's okay to meet someone and tell them the very next instant, that you really like their company. It's okay to be a stubborn optimist and it's okay if you don't watch news cause that makes so sad. Its okay to be passionate - and no, you're not trying too hard. It's okay to be labelled; only to prove it wrong. It's absolutely okay to choose what you want, even if it looks nothing like what's being chosen around you.You get only so many years to live in your one mind and your one body. Embrace that and make this little time you have on this planet worth it.
