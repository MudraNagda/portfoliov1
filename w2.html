// befriend //

they are coming -
a thousand feet
a million sounds
the swish of wind
i hear
restless leaves
on the ground -
and i know
if I run fast enough
i will survive
at the cost
of my sanity
[if you run once
you run forever]

but tonight
i will rest;
slow down
in this race
against time -
my worst friend;
sit with my fears
take a breath
from the heavy nothingness
of this dark air
[the calm
before the storm]

i will sit
with them
stroke their face
soak the ugliness of it all
[if you love love, you've got to love them all]

for this night
i am engulfed
in those feelings
that have chased for years
insecurities
projection
pushing out
to the boundaries
of your brain
the feeling of "not doing/being enough"
[i like to pretend
we have it all.
do we.
play pretend
can only carry you so far]

and i think
i know it all
and i think
i know how to deal
with feelings i haven't felt yet
[does anyone?
we have so much to learn
start with yourself]

but so, tonight
i learn
let pragmatism and
overdramatization
win over sheer optimism
[only tonight;
when the
morning comes
i will come back to myself;
i know better of nothing else
than to love like the morning sun]

tonight is
to be soft
with my worst fears
and converse
[i don't have to feel it;
just observe]

and then
trap them
from haunting me again
[there will be new ones
to take their place]

in this broken poem
i pretend is art.
[anything you create
is art
just find the right pair
of eyes,
or hands;
and look,
feel]
