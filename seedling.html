Seedling is our Final Year Research Project. It explores a gamified approach to plant education for children by using research in the fields of Persuasive Technology, Gamification and Behavioural Science. Our aim is to use the positive persuasive power of technology to establish a connection between children(and the Gen Z) and plants.
