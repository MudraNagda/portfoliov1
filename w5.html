// sun talk //

It'll startle you in myriad ways
showing up when you least expect it
in its huge warm coat
adorned with every color of nature
it'll show up
through broken doors of trust -
broken but still hope to function
creaky wooden floors -

every conscious step you take will be loud, blunt
dusty ceilings -
dust is energy and energy is enough
It'll teach you
to stride like the wind
and prance like a sun beam
Cause the sun, the sun
it'll show up
every single time
with magnificence
cause it knows of nothing else
but to spark the soul it fell upon -
the universe works in magical ways
and the next time it shows up
say hello and
share the magic over a hot cup.
